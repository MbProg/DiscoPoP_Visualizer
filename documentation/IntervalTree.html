Class: IntervalTree

IntervalTree

Implementation of a Red-Black-Tree augmented for having intervals as node-keys. Based on chapters 13 and 14 from book: Introduction to Algorithms, Third Edition (By: Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest and Clifford Stein)
